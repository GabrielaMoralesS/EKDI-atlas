# Limitations

- EKDI is a decision-support atlas and does not confirm presence, absence, extinction, or rediscovery.
- The public browser app visualizes processed Atlantic Forest outputs and does not recompute the full EKDI index client-side.
- Full scientific recomputation requires external or locally generated intermediate inputs that are not bundled in the public repository.
- Plant candidates and Knowledge Ghosts are evidence signals, not complete floristic inventories or official threat categories.
- Forest remaining and post-record loss provide habitat context, but they do not by themselves prove species persistence or absence.
- Atlantic Forest weights are a case-study preset and should be recalibrated before applying EKDI to another biome.
