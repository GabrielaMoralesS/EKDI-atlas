# 90-Second Demo Video Script

## 0–10s: Problem

Biodiversity records preserve ecological memory, but the landscape around those records may change.

## 10–25s: Map And Critical Gaps

EKDI maps where botanical knowledge may need field verification. Critical Gaps show cells where old evidence, habitat change and knowledge deficit converge.

## 25–40s: Why This Cell

Clicking a cell opens the evidence panel. EKDI shows the reasoning behind the priority, including sampling antiquity, post-record forest loss and richness deficit when available.

## 40–55s: Plant Candidates / Botanical Memory

When species-level evidence is available, Plant Candidates and Botanical Memory profiles help experts decide what needs voucher review, taxonomic validation or field checking.

## 55–70s: Expedition Planner

Selected cells and species can be added to the Expedition Planner and exported as CSV or evidence JSON for field planning.

## 70–85s: Data Integrity / Replication

The dashboard documents evidence completeness, limitations and how EKDI can be adapted to other biomes.

## 85–90s: Closing

EKDI turns open biodiversity records into transparent questions for science, field verification and conservation.
